My favorite place in NCAT: Desse Clock tower

This is Desse Clock tower in NCAT. It is about 85 feet tall.
It has two clocks :one its north face and the other south.
It also has a bell system that will chime on the hour.
During the night time this place glows and files your heart with excitement.
I always take awalk to this place and refresh myself.

My favorite place in Greensboro: Barber Park

This is Barber Park in Greensboro. It has both indoor and outdoor activities where you can have a wonderful time with friends and families.
The indoor sports like tennis, basketball and vollyball are offered throughout the year regardless of the weather.
What I like more about this park is the one mile walking trail and the free 18-hole disc golf course.
It is such a wonderful venue for celebrating birthday parties and weddings.
This park also have some more features and amenities like picnic shelters , playground and Tot Spot and Sprayground.
